Editor's Letter
01
"Wealth is not about accumulation. It is the freedom to chase the horizon — whether that's on open water or a midnight dance floor."

There exists a rare breed of individual for whom the boundaries between work and play have long since dissolved. They pilot their own course — literally and figuratively — navigating from the crystalline waters of the Caribbean to the velvet-dark lounges of the world's most storied nightlife destinations.

This inaugural issue of Forbes MX1 captures that spirit. We followed our subjects through sun-soaked days aboard luxury sport fishing vessels, where the thrill of the catch is rivaled only by the endless blue stretching to the horizon. And then we followed them into the night — where conversations flow as freely as the cocktails, and every encounter holds the promise of something extraordinary.

These are not profiles of wealth. They are portraits of a life lived at full throttle — unapologetic, unfiltered, and unmistakably alive. Welcome to MX1. This is where the story begins.
